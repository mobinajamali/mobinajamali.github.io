About Me!

As a machine learning practitioner, I bring over three years of expertise in machine learning, computer vision , and robotics to the table. Graduating from the University of Calgary with a BSc in Physics, my academic journey propelled me to the forefront of innovation. During my thesis work, I pushed the boundaries of control in Highly Maneuverable Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (HM-AUVs), honing my skills in automation and control

Now, I'm all about mixing tech with imagination. I want to create robots that really get what's going on around them and can interact smoothly. I love leading cool projects and keeping up with what's hot in the industry. Plus, I'm big on writing code that's ready for prime time. I'm into building big systems for training and putting machine learning solutions into action. Let's team up and make some seriously smart robots!
