About me!

Welcome to my portfolio website! Explore the diverse range of projects I have worked on and don't hesitate to reach out to me for any inquiries or collaboration opportunities.

I'm a Robotics Software Developer with a passionate quest to infuse robots with a touch of human-like intelligence. By harnessing the capabilities of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, I'm dedicated to crafting software solutions that empower robots to acquire knowledge, adapt to their surroundings, and engage with the world.

I recently graduated from the University of Calgary with BSc in Physics, and while working on my thesis, I advanced the control of a Highly Maneuverable Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (HM-AUV), which allowed me to gain valuable experiences in automation and control. With the convergence of technology and imagination, my objective is to engineer robots that possess a profound understanding of their environment and exhibit seamless interaction, enabling them to play a pivotal role in unraveling the mysteries of the unknown.
