As a machine learning practitioner, I have over three years of expertise in machine learning, computer vision , and robotics. Graduating from the University of Calgary with a BSc in Physics, my academic journey has laid the groundwork for a career dedicated to innovation. During my thesis, I worked on developing advanced architectures for automation and control of Highly Maneuverable Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (HM-AUVs), leading to a deepened understanding of automation and control strategies.

Now, I'm all about mixing tech with imagination. I'm interested in ways we can allow robots to work alongside humans to perform complex, multi-step tasks, using a combination of learning and planning. I enjoy production quality coding, developing large-scale training pipelines, and deploying ML solutions in production. Let's team up and make some seriously smart robots!
