HM-AUV Control

Well, in this project for my undergraduate thesis, I created the algorithms for the motion planning of the AUV below, with six degrees of freedom, and I was able to successfully implement and simulate the outcomes.

Wordle
Game Project

Prepare yourself to enter the world of words! Online word game, Wordle, recently gained a lot of popularity and was added to the NYT Games website. Players are given six chances to guess a five-letter word. For each guess, players receive feedback in the form of coloured tiles that show which letters match or are in the right place. I basically wrote the entire algorithm from scratch for this project, drawing inspiration from Pytopia. Have fun playing!
