Mobina Jamali

I'm a roboticist and AI researcher working on Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning.

I received my BSc in Physics in 2023 from the University of Calgary, focusing on developing advanced architectures for automation and control of Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) advised by Alex Ramirez Serrano. After graduation, I started my self learning journey in the ever growing world of Artificial Intelligence. I completed a MicroMasters program in Data Science from the University of California SanDiego, learning about mathematical and applied sides of data science, while polishing my robotics knowledge by taking courses in The Construct platform .

I've been working on my side projects as well as collaborating with different universities to develop agents capable of working alongside humans to perform complex, multi-step tasks, using a combination of learning and planning. I joined Intelligent Robot Learning Lab to work on developing intelligent agents capable of collaborating with other agents, advised by Matthew E. Taylor.
